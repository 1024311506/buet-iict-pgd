Welcome to BUET IICT Postgraduate Diploma (PG.Dip.) in Business IT Program

Postgraduate Diploma Admission

The applications will be scrutinized by the Institute and a list of eligible candidates will be displayed on the Institute’s notice board and website. Candidates will have to appear in tests conducted by the Selection Committee of the Institute.

The admission test will be held on the date published by the Institute. On the basis of the result of the test, a list of preliminarily selected candidates will be published.

Admission Test

The MCQ test for the PG.Dip. (ICT) program generally takes place in the months of April and/or October every year.

The test will consist of 60 multiple-choice questions divided into three sections:

  • Section 1: IQ – 25 questions
  • Section 2: English – 10 questions
  • Section 3: Computer Fundamentals – 25 questions

Note: A candidate must pass each section individually to qualify.
