Facilities

Now is an era of Information and Communication Technology (ICT). The dream of the country is poverty reduction and economic growth by creating a knowledge-driven economy and society based on ICT. To realize this goal, our university graduates must possess strong ICT knowledge.

Therefore, it is essential to establish robust ICT infrastructure in every university and create an environment of teaching, learning, and research in ICT. IICT has the following facilities to support this vision:

Access to the Internet

IICT has established an Internet Browsing Room for students, recognizing that learning today extends beyond books to digital resources such as research papers and e-books. The Internet facility provides access to 28 international consortiums, including:

  • American Institute of Physics
  • IEEE
  • Optical Society of America
  • American Society for Civil Engineers
  • Springer
  • And many others

Laboratory Facility

IICT maintains several laboratories to support practical and experimental learning. These include:

  • Communication Laboratory
  • Embedded System Laboratory
  • Network Laboratory
  • Multimedia and Graphics Laboratory
  • Hardware Laboratory

Classroom Facility

The Institute has three modern PC classrooms, each equipped with computers, multimedia projectors, and audiovisual systems. These classrooms are used by postgraduate students of IICT and by other departments for undergraduate courses.

Additionally, IICT has two large lecture halls with multimedia facilities, each accommodating over 50 students.

Library Facility

IICT has a well-equipped library for teachers and students. It contains modern ICT-related books, rare foundational texts, up-to-date journals, and research papers in IT-related fields.

Seminar / Workshop

IICT regularly organizes national and international seminars featuring renowned experts from various scientific fields. These events help students and faculty stay updated on modern developments and exchange ideas.

Research Papers / Journals

IICT actively encourages teachers and students to publish research papers in national and international journals and conferences. Students conducting thesis work under faculty guidance are often able to publish their research as conference or journal papers.
