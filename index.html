BUET IICT

Postgraduate Diploma in Business IT

A 24‑credit-hour program bridging business strategy and information technology.

Program Overview

The PGD in Business IT is a credit‑based program offered by IICT at BUET, designed for professionals and graduates aiming to blend business acumen with IT skills.

Admission Criteria

  • Four‑year degree in Engineering, Mathematics, Physics, Statistics, BBA, or equivalent.
  • Minimum GPA 3.50 (out of 5.00) or first division in SSC & HSC (or equivalent).
  • At least 50% marks or GPA 2.50 out of 4.0 in the bachelor’s degree.

Format & Credit Structure

  • Total Credits: 24 credit hours
  • Full‑Time: 12–15 credits/term; no external employment unless TA/RA; employment mid‑term allowed with approval.
  • Part‑Time: Up to 9 credits/term; requires employer’s written consent.
