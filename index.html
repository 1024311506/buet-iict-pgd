Welcome to BUET IICT Postgraduate Diploma (PG.Dip.) in Business IT Program

The Institute of Information and Communication Technology (IICT) at BUET offers a Postgraduate Diploma (PG.Dip.) in Business IT.

Eligibility Criteria

  1. Academic Background: The applicant must hold a four-year Bachelor's degree in one of the following:
    • B.Sc. in Engineering
    • B.Sc. in Mathematics, Physics, or Statistics
    • BBA or an equivalent degree from any recognized university
  2. Secondary & Higher Secondary Requirements: Minimum GPA of 3.50 out of 5.00 or first division in both SSC and HSC or equivalent exams.
  3. Undergraduate Requirements: At least 50% marks or a GPA of 2.50 out of 4.00 in the four-year Bachelor's degree.

Program Structure

The minimum requirement for the PG.Dip. degree is 24 credit hours.

  • Can take up to 9 credit hours per term.

Switching Between Full-Time and Part-Time

Students may switch between full-time and part-time status before the start of a semester or term, with the recommendation of the respective BPGS or RAC.
